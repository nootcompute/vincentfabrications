2022 Closet Showing(2)

In December 2022, Vincent Meyrick created their first independent fashion collection of over twenty separately-designed and handmade items. This collection was presented in a self-produced fashion show in collaboration with two other fashion designers, Grace Dinham and Jasmine Featherston (Feath). Titled Closet Collection, the group collaboration brought together shoemaking, knitting, and tailoring in a theatrical and extravagant catwalk, in which models walked out in their underwear and dressed into their looks in front of a live audience.

Before the runway, fittings were held at a studio in the Nicholas Building.
