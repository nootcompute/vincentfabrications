2024 Book Bags
and other old stuff

After relocating to a new studio in Brunswick, Vincent Fabrications stages a test shoot with models Joey and Anise in order to present the book bags. The set includes a sculptural work made by friend and collaborator Tyke Rashid. Joey and Anise stand by a central piece constructed from wooden planks and 3D printed joinery. A range of clothing, constructed hard objects, interior artifacts and bags blend together cohesively through a digital lense.
