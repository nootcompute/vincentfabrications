Vincent Meyrick, the founder of the label Vincent Fabrications, is an emerging designer, tailor and knitwear technician from Melbourne, Australia. Vincent grew up in theatres, around creative arts practitioners and performers. Their father is a theatre director and their mother is a period costume designer. Theatricality lies at the heart of their work. In the past, they have produced collections based around the intersection of old-world tailoring and contemporary workwear. After training in the knitwear factories of northern Japan in 2022 to better understand textile production, Vincent has returned to Melbourne to continue their work as a designer. Their select offerings and collections reflect ideas of utility and formal dress.

Vincent Fabrications operates on the lands of the Wurundjeri people of the Kulin nation in so-called ‘Australia’. We acknowledge the traditional custodians of these lands and pay our respects to their elder’s past, present, and emerging. We recognise that sovereignty was never ceded.
